What is kratom?

Kratom (Mitragyna speciosa) is a tropical evergreen tree in the coffee family, native to Southeast Asia, where its leaves have been used traditionally for centuries. Products made from kratom leaf contain naturally occurring alkaloids, the most abundant of which is mitragynine. Dandy products are made from kratom leaf in two formats: gummies made with leaf extract, and raw leaf powder. We label the mitragynine content of every product and we don't make any claims about effects or benefits.

Are your products lab tested?

Yes — every batch, by an independent third-party laboratory. Each batch is tested for alkaloid content (mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine) before it's released for sale. Every package carries a batch number, and current Certificates of Analysis are available to download on our Lab Results page — COAs for any past batch are available on request.

What is your age requirement?

You must be 21 or older to purchase from Dandy — no exceptions. Our site asks you to confirm your age on entry, our checkout requires you to affirm you are 21+, and our products are labeled accordingly. Keep all kratom products out of reach of children.
