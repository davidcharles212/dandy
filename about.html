About Dandy

A botanical brand built on a simple idea: label it honestly, test it independently, and stand behind every batch.

Dandy is the house brand of Ollie Botanical Group LLC, an American botanical wellness company. We started Dandy because we kept seeing the same problem across the kratom category: inconsistent labeling, vague sourcing, and products you couldn't trace back to a test result. We thought a kratom brand should operate the way any good consumer brand does — with clear labels, published standards, and someone who answers the phone.

How we make our products

Every Dandy product is manufactured in partnership with an FDA-registered facility that operates under current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP). Raw material is identity-verified on arrival, blended and filled under documented procedures, and packed with a batch number that follows it through the entire chain.

Tested by people who don't work for us

Every batch — not a representative sample, every batch — goes to an independent, third-party laboratory before it's released for sale. We test for alkaloid content, so the milligrams on the label match the milligrams in the product. Certificates of Analysis for current batches are available to download, and COAs for any past batch are available on request. You can read more on our Lab Results page.

What we won't say

We describe our products plainly: what they are, what's in them, and how they were made. We don't make health or medical claims, and we're wary of anyone in this category who does. Our products are for adults 21 and over, and we only ship to jurisdictions where kratom is legal.

Our word

If something isn't right with your order, we'll make it right — that's the whole policy, and the fine print on our Refund & Return page just spells out the details. We're a small team, we read every email, and we'd rather lose a sale than lose your trust.
