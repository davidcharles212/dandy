Lab results

Every batch we sell is tested by an independent, third-party laboratory before release. Not a sample of batches — every batch.

What we test for

  • Alkaloid content — mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine, so the label matches what's inside.
  • Heavy metals — lead, arsenic, cadmium, and mercury, against USP limits.
  • Microbials — total plate count, yeast and mold, E. coli, and salmonella.
  • Pesticides — a broad residue panel appropriate for botanical raw materials.

A batch that fails any panel is rejected and never enters our inventory. Test results are tied to the batch number printed on every pouch and bottle.
